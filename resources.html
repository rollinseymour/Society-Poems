Getting help

Society Poems is a place for peer support — people listening to people. It isn't a crisis service. If things feel like too much right now, the resources below are here for you, day or night.

⚠ If you're in immediate danger

Please call your local emergency number right away (for example 911 in the US, 999 in the UK, 112 across the EU, 000 in Australia). Your life matters.
