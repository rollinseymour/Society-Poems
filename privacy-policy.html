Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2025

Thank you for visiting Society Poems. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our website.

Information we collect

We collect information in a few different ways:

  • Information you provide directly:
    • Account information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and username.
    • User content: We collect the stories, poems, writings, and other content you voluntarily upload to our service.
    • Feedback information: When you use our feedback form, we collect the message you send along with your username and email so we can follow up.
  • Information from third-party services:
    • Google authentication: If you choose to sign in with Google, we receive your name and email address from Google to create or access your account.
  • Information we collect automatically:
    • Log data: Like most websites, we may collect information that your browser sends whenever you visit our site, such as your IP address, browser type, and the pages you visit. This data is used for security and operational purposes.

How we use your information

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the Society Poems website.
  • Allow you to create and manage your account.
  • Display content you submit to the community, with your username — or as "Anonymous" if you choose.
  • Process your feedback and reports to keep the community safe.
  • Protect the security and integrity of our website.

How we share your information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information in the following circumstances:

  • Publicly: Your username and the content you choose to upload will be publicly visible. If you post anonymously, your username is not shown with that post.
  • With service providers: We use third-party services like Firebase (database and authentication) and Google (authentication and email) that may process your data on our behalf.
  • For legal reasons: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to a valid request by public authorities.

Your data rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete the information we have about you. You can delete your uploaded content or your entire account through the website's features. If you need assistance, please contact us.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.

Contact us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at officialsocietypoems@gmail.com.
